Fan-Favorite Episodes

Bluey has so many memorable episodes that bring laughs, smiles, and even a few tears. Here are just a few:

  • “Grannies”

    • Bluey and Bingo dress up as two elderly “grannies” who cause chaos in the house! This episode celebrates imagination and the joy of make-believe.

  • “Magic Xylophone”

    • Bluey finds a magical xylophone that can freeze her dad in place. This episode explores the concept of power, boundaries, and sharing.

  • “Camping”

    • In this heartfelt episode, Bluey befriends a French-speaking boy while on a family camping trip. They overcome language barriers to form a meaningful friendship, highlighting the importance of connection and inclusion.
