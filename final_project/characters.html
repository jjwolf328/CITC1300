Meet the Characters of Bluey

Main Characters

Get to know the heart of Bluey! Each character brings something unique to the series, making it relatable and fun for all ages.

  • Bluey

    Bluey is a six-year-old blue heeler pup who is curious, playful, and full of energy. Her vivid imagination leads her into all kinds of creative games, adventures, and discoveries. Whether she’s playing “Grannies” with her sister or learning a new game with her dad, Bluey’s excitement and creativity make her a joy to watch.

  • Bingo

    Bluey’s four-year-old sister, Bingo, is sweet, thoughtful, and sometimes a bit more sensitive than her big sister. She has a strong imagination of her own and loves joining Bluey on all her escapades. Bingo’s relationship with Bluey is heartwarming and relatable to any sibling relationship.

  • Dad (Bandit)

    Bandit, Bluey and Bingo’s dad, is known for his playfulness and quick wit. He loves getting involved in the girls' games, often playing the “villain” or taking on silly roles to make the girls laugh. But Bandit also imparts valuable life lessons in his fun-loving way, making him a favorite among parents and kids alike.

  • Mum (Chilli)

    Chilli, the girls' mum, balances work with family life, and her wisdom and warmth ground the show. She’s often the one to help Bluey and Bingo understand their emotions and guide them in life’s little lessons, making her a comforting presence on screen.

Friends and Family

Meet Bluey’s friends Coco, Mackenzie, Snickers, and more! Each friend has a unique personality, adding to Bluey’s adventures with their own quirks and imaginations. Bluey’s extended family, like Nana and Uncle Stripe, also make appearances, sharing fun and humor with the whole family.
