Welcome to the World of Bluey!

Step into the world of Bluey, the award-winning children’s show that has captured the hearts of families worldwide. Created in Australia, Bluey follows the daily adventures of a lovable six-year-old blue heeler named Bluey, along with her sister Bingo and her parents, Bandit and Chilli.

About the Show

Since its debut, Bluey has become a beloved staple for families, known for its rich storytelling, humor, and heartwarming portrayal of family life. Bluey isn’t just a show for kids—it’s a series that explores life’s everyday moments in ways that parents and children alike can connect with. Each episode brings to life a new adventure that inspires play, kindness, and creativity.

Award-Winning and Loved Worldwide

Winner of multiple awards for children’s programming, Bluey has been praised for its:

  • Relatable characters

  • Family-oriented stories

  • Gentle, thoughtful lessons on emotions, friendships, and family bonds.
